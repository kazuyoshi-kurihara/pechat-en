How to connect Chappet and your smartphone

Can't connect to Chappet?

Is Chappet turned on?
Press and hold the power button on the backside of Chappet for three seconds to turn the power on. If Chappet is out of battery, please charge it using the provided charging cable.
Are you close enough to Chappet?
Make sure you are within 10 meters (without any obstacles) from Chappet.
Is Chappet connected to another smartphone?
Chappet can only be connected to one smartphone at a time. Disconnect Chappet from the paired smartphone and try again.
Is your smartphone connected to other Bluetooth devices?
Please disconnect other Bluetooth devices paired with your smartphone when using Chappet.
Are you near a microwave or other equipment that emits electromagnetic waves?
Please use Chappet away from equipment that emits electromagnectic waves.
Does your smartphone/tablet support HFP?
Some features may not be used on smartphones/tablets that do not support HFP.

How to Use the Chappet Device

How to Use the App

About adding phrases

  • Adding categories/phrases

    You can newly add phrase categories. You can also add phrases by entering text or by recording your voice.

  • Adding songs

    You can add new songs by recording your own. You can also download additional songs from the store.

  • Adding stories

    You can add new stories by recording your own. You can also download additional stories from the store.

About editing phrases

  • Deleting your history

    You can delete your history.

  • Editing phrases

    You can reorder items. You can also change titles/categories, adjust voice pitch for items added by voice and delete categories/phrases.
    *Only categories or phrases you added.

  • Editing songs

    You can reorder items. You can also change titles, adjust voice pitch and delete songs.
    *Only songs you added.

  • Editing stories

    You can reorder items. You can also change titles, adjust voice pitch, and delete stories.
    *Only stories you added.
